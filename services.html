Services

Technical SEO Audit

A thorough crawl-based audit of your site's technical health — covering everything that search engines use to evaluate, index, and rank your pages. You get a prioritized action plan with specific fixes, not a 200-item spreadsheet with no sense of what matters. I've run these for enterprise sites and early-stage startups alike; the fundamentals don't change.

  • Crawl error analysis
  • Indexing audit & fix plan
  • Site architecture review
  • Internal linking analysis
  • Canonicalization & redirect audit
  • Core Web Vitals assessment
  • Structured data / schema review
  • Prioritized recommendations

On-Site Optimization

Keyword research and mapping, title tag and meta data rewrites, internal linking restructure, content gap analysis, and on-page recommendations that align your site's structure with how people actually search. I write directly when needed — but my focus is on the architecture and optimization layer, not content volume.

  • Keyword research & mapping
  • Title tag & meta data optimization
  • Internal linking strategy
  • Content gap & opportunity analysis
  • EEAT signal assessment
  • On-page content recommendations
  • URL structure review

Reporting & Analytics Setup

Most organic reporting setups answer the wrong questions. I configure GA4 and Google Search Console correctly from the start — or audit and repair an existing setup — and build dashboards and reporting frameworks that connect search performance to business outcomes. Good data infrastructure is the difference between reactive and proactive SEO.

  • GA4 setup & configuration
  • GSC setup & property verification
  • Conversion tracking
  • Looker / Data Studio dashboards
  • Keyword & ranking tracking
  • Monthly performance reporting
  • Stakeholder-ready reporting templates

Organic Growth Strategy

For clients who need more than a one-time fix — an ongoing strategic partnership where I analyze performance data, identify the highest-leverage opportunities, and build roadmaps that make organic growth predictable. This is the work I do full-time at the senior level, available to consulting clients in a focused format.

  • Competitive landscape analysis
  • Organic growth roadmap
  • Prioritization framework
  • Monthly strategy calls
  • Ongoing performance analysis
  • GEO / AI search optimization
